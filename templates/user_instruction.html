User Guide

Welcome to Wallet Notes! Here are some common questions and answers to help you get started.

To add a new transaction, go to the Detail page and click the Add button. Fill in the required fields (date, category, type, amount, etc.) and click Save.

Go to the Statistics page. You can view your income, expenses, and balance in the form of charts and tables. Use the filters to view data by month or year.

Go to the Management page. Here, you can add, edit, or delete income/expense categories, set budgets, and manage currencies and accounts.

On the Detail page, click the Export button. Your transaction data will be downloaded as a CSV file.

Please use the Contact Us page to send us an email, and we will address your feedback as soon as possible.
